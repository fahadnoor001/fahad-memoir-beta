The Journey

Year 1

First Steps

Walked into the gym with nothing but curiosity and a chip on my shoulder. Started learning the big three: squat, bench, deadlift.

Year 2

Finding the Platform

Entered my first competition. The nerves, the adrenaline, the community — I was hooked. Started training with purpose and programming.

Year 3

Breaking Through

Hit milestone PRs across all three lifts. Learned that strength isn't just physical — it's the mental game that separates good from great.

Present

The Ongoing Pursuit

Still chasing numbers, still pushing limits. The barbell remains the most honest mirror I've ever looked into.

Discipline Over Motivation

Motivation gets you to the gym once. Discipline gets you there every day at 5 AM when it's cold, dark, and your body is screaming for rest.

Trust the Process

PRs don't happen overnight. They're built in the monotony of consistent training, proper nutrition, and showing up when nobody's watching.

Iron Sharpens Iron

The powerlifting community is built on lifting each other up — literally and figuratively. Your training partners become your family.
