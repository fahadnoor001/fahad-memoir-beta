Biography

Born and raised in Houston, Texas — a city that teaches you grit before it teaches you anything else. The humidity, the hustle, the culture — it all shaped who I am.

Growing up in Houston meant growing up at the intersection of ambition and diversity. From the sprawling energy corridors to the tight-knit neighborhoods, every corner of this city left its mark. It's where I learned that hard work isn't something you talk about — it's something you live.

Houston didn't just raise me. It built the foundation for everything that came after: the discipline I found in the gym, the curiosity that drove me through college, and the relentless drive to build something meaningful.
